Featured Project

mmWave Four-Element MIMO Antenna Design Verification

Electronics Research Institute (ERI) | Jun 2024 - Jul 2024

During my training at the ERI, I verified the design of a mmWave Four-Element MIMO Antenna for 5G systems. This involved detailed design, simulation, and performance analysis.

Project Steps:

  • Designing a Single Antenna Structure: Used CST Studio Suite to replicate the research paper design with accurate dimensions and materials.
  • Simulation and Analysis: Evaluated S-parameters, 2D/3D radiation patterns, gain, and efficiency to validate performance.
  • MIMO Configuration: Expanded the single antenna into a four-element MIMO array. MATLAB was used to analyze mutual coupling and isolation.

Key Achievements:

  • Successfully replicated and evaluated the single antenna structure.
  • Designed a robust four-element MIMO antenna with optimized isolation.
  • Gained practical experience in antenna design, simulation, and MIMO systems.
  • Contributed to 5G system research and verification.
